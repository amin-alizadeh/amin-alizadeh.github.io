Occupation or position held Developer
Main activities and responsibilities

Delicode Oy Ltd. is a game developer startup in Finland developing games using depth cameras, Microsoft Kinect.

Currently I am developing interactive games using the depth camera for the young audience as well as other research projects in similar fields. The projects require patience and great mathematical skills. I was doing a research and developing algorithms to recognize gestures using the depth camera device. The machine learning algorithms guesses which gesture was performed by the user based on the pre-defined list of gestures. The gestures are specialized for the kids and I used this project for my master’s thesis at the Department of Computer Science at the University of Helsinki.

My main responsibilities:

Developing interactive games;
Translating human movements into operable commands;
Optimization problems and implementations;
Developing machine learning algorithms to recognize the gestures;
Defining test cases and developing 3D games to test the accuracy of the algorithms;
Using the results of the research project in interactive games.
